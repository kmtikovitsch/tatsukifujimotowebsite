EARLY LIFE

Fujimoto was born on October 10th, 1992. He was born in Japan, where he still resides.
At a young age he had a passion for drawing and would attend painting classes with
his grandparents. His passion would take him through college, obtaining a degree in
"Western Painting" from Tohoku University in 2014.

CAREER

He began to write small one-shot stories and submit them to Shounen Jump,
where they would go on to win some awards and gain popularity. After serveral
small stories, he eventually went on to getting one of his works serialized.
Fire Punch, his first major series, ran from 2016, to 2018, creating exposure and
polularity to his name. Not soon after the ending of his first series, his most well
known manga began. Chainsaw man, one of the most popular series to date,
began publication ten months after the ending of Fire Punch. Chainsaw Man
is currently Fujimoto's longest running series, still currently being written and
published. Between illustrating Chainsaw Man, he's also created a handful of
short published stories and collaborations.
