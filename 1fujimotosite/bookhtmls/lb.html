Look Back is a beautiful story following two young artists. Fujino is a girl who has the desire to be the best manga artist out there, but cannot draw backgrounds. Kyomoto, a shut-in student, has the skills that Fujino needs for her story. They then began to work on their story, as the book follows their life together as manga authors.

This is Fujimoto's best work other than his man series Chainsaw Man. Look Back is a heartwarming and yet absolutly gut-wrenching, heartbreaking story with very lovable characters, fantastic pacing, and a very simple plot to follow. The writing is absolutely perfect and this story is very accesable to almost everyone who enjoys a little sadness and drama.
Rating: 5/5
