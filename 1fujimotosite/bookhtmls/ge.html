Goodbye Eri follows a young boy who has an interest in making goofy movies and filming. He makes an unlikely friend who adores his films and wants him to keep going and make more. Although this sounds like a normal story, things take a dark twist that none expect.

This book tugs at the heartstrings as it isn't your average Fujimoto action story. It has very realisitc events and characters, making them all enjoyable and relatable. The pacing for this book is perfect for its length, and the plot is pretty straightforward and easy to grasp. This story is very short and a down to earth read
Rating: 4/5
