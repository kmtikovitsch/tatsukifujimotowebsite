The story of Fire Punch follows the story of Agni, a man with the power of regeneration. He is on a hunt to avenge the brutal murder of his sister, all the while contantly being a walking torch.

This story starts off strong, but gets confusing to follow and falls off at the end. This book is for very mature audiences and contains very graphic themes. Overall it's the most difficult manga to read out of all the Fujimoto works. The characters and plot are very likable, but the plot and some events are so disturbing that it jumbles parts of the story. The pacing is very fast as well and can be a little confusing at times.
Rating: 2.5/5
