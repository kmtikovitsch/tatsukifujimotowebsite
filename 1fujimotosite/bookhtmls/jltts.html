This story is sort of a typical love story following a boy who is writing a song for a girl he likes. When he makes his music video for her, weird things begin to happen. Supernatural things begin to insue while the video is played and brings global attention to the song, but not for the reasons you think.

This story is a very easy and calm read compared to Fujimoto's other works. This book is for everyone, the pacing is perfect and the characters and plot are very easy to follow and connect to. This is a short one-shot in collaberation with Oto Toda, the author of To Strip the Flesh, another amazing read.
Rating: 2/5
